Hangzhou Jingo Technology Co., Ltd. takes the leads to set off a new round of technological transformation for monetary settlement system in the world. It makes use of advanced technology of modern network modularization, and combines it with financial settlement system of distributed account book. In that way, the traditional idea of cash on delivery can be modified. Besides, “commodity”and “currency”can be automatically distributed. By means of reducing and cutting the time of commodity circulation and monetary settlement, it can increase social energy consumption, and further liberate social productivity. “Hangzhou Jingo Technology Co., Ltd.” has developed an APP called “VV”, which is an intelligent cellphone social network software with complete set of service and advanced performance; not only can it be directly connected to  jingomall.com, an online trade platform in China which subordinates to Jingo, but also it covers various commodities in the world and allows for direct transaction as well. Such online and offline sales mode focusing on commodities not only provides customers with access to O2O experience stores, but also ties consumers’ consumption habits, social insurance and credit system together. “VV”APP is also embedded with the most advanced monetary settlement service system in the world. It integrates traditional bank transferring and bank loaning, etc. into moneybag project, which makes one-touch transferring and password-free payment become a normal social phenomenon in the future business world. Game software in moneybag such as casting (golden) beans, and planting golden beans, etc. can take modern people away from their busy work simply by games like weeding, catching insects, watering and fertilizing, etc. These games are not only interesting, but also a way to accumulate money. Breeding bean sprouts under outdoor sunshine can lead users go outside for exercises and improve their physical health, and, it can make users feel the real experience of hard working and perspiration in this gold-digging games. Hangzhou Jingo Technology Co., Ltd. is not just limited to its current achievements, there is also space left for software development in the future. With the proceeding of research and development, we will add customer transaction and monetary settlement system for supermarket, shopping mall to “VV”APP. In the future, as long as users get “VV” APP installed on their phone, they can experience automatic payment and financial settlement in shopping mall and supermarket under hardware support. "Jingomall.com (JINGO·China)" is a network trading platform for global customer service under the flag of Hangzhou Jinge Technology Co., Ltd. The transaction contains all kinds of social goods including financial products. For the convenience of (global) customers’ online commodity trading, Jinge developed cloud computing data systems and mobile client systems.

Currency·Commodity Distributary Technology is known as the world's most complex, most profound financial theory, it can break trade barriers of countries and facilitate commodity circulation, reduce commodity trading time and the cost of currency exchange. The Chain Sales Model that commodity is the point where the sales happen is known as the Internet of things 3.0 Technology, which can link personal credit to consumers’ spending habits. These two kinds of technologies integrate each other deeply in the trading platform, which not only subverts the original commodity circulation model, but also is in line with modern people’s psychological service needs that more convenience, more refinement is required.
